{% include "head.html" ignore missing %} {% include "optimization.html" ignore missing %} \begin{align} \max_{q_d,q_c,q_{ru},q_{rd}} \sum_t \big( \lambda(t) q_d(t) - \lambda(t)q_c(t) + \lambda_{ru}(t) q_{ru}(t) + \lambda_{rd}(t) q_{rd}(t) + \lambda(t) \delta_{ru}(t)q_{ru}(t) - \lambda(t)\delta_{rd}(t)q_{rd}(t) \big) e^{-tR} \end{align}
subject to the following constraints
\begin{align} s(t+1) = \eta_s s(t) + \eta_c q_c(t) & - q_d(t) + \eta_c\delta_{rd}(t) q_{rd}(t) - \delta_{ru}(t) q_{ru}(t) \\ 0 \leq s(t) & \leq \bar Q \\ q_c(t) + q_d(t) + q_{ru}(t) + q_{rd}(t) & \leq \bar Q \\ q_c(t) + q_{rd}(t) & \leq \bar P \\ q_d(t) + q_{ru}(t) & \leq \bar P \end{align}
The total revenue can be calculated as
\begin{align} r_\text{arb} & = \sum_t \lambda(t)\big(q_d(t) - q_c(t) + \delta_{ru}(t) q_{ru}(t) - \delta_{rd}(t) q_{rd}(t)\big) \\ r_\text{reg} & = \sum_t \lambda_{ru}(t) q_{ru}(t) + \lambda_{rd}(t)q_{rd}(t) \\ r & = r_\text{arb} + r_\text{reg} \end{align}
The data required to run this analysis in ERCOT are the day ahead LMP, reg up price, and the reg down price.

NOTE: If the energy storage device is not participating in frequency regulation, \( q_{ru}(t) \) and \( q_{rd}(t) \) are equal to zero for all \( t \).
